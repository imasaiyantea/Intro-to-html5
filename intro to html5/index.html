What is Genshin Impact?

Genshin Impact is an open-world RGP game developed by Hoyoverse (formally known as miHoyo). Players can obtain an array of different characters, using them on their journey to battle against all kinds of enemies and explore the fascinating world of Teyvat!

Main attributes

  • Huge, beautiful open world
  • Element-based fighting system
  • More than 60 characters with unique abilities
